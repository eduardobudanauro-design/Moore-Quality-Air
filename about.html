Our Story

How Moore Quality Air Began

Jonathan McDaniel started Moore Quality Air in 2013. After earning a bachelor's degree in business administration at the University of New Mexico — concentrating on entrepreneurial studies — and working in the HVAC industry at several local companies, Jon set out to build a heating, cooling and plumbing company that Albuquerque homeowners could truly trust.

In 2015, Anthony Hughes partnered with Jon. A former Naval aircraft technician with an associate's degree focused on HVAC-Refrigeration, Anthony brought a wealth of technical expertise, pride and professionalism that became the backbone of the company. Together, Jon and Anthony lead the technical work and the team behind Moore Quality Air.

Our approach is simple: show up on time, explain what we are doing, do the job right, and come back if something is not right. Over the years, that approach has earned Moore Quality Air a reputation across Albuquerque, Rio Rancho, Corrales and Los Lunas as the team people call first and recommend to their neighbors.

Every Technician on Our Team Is Journeyman Licensed

That is not something every HVAC and plumbing company in Albuquerque can say. Every technician who walks into your home carries a journeyman license — they are trained, tested and qualified to do the work correctly. Jon and Anthony lead the technical work, so when you call Moore Quality Air, you get professionals, not apprentices.

As a Rheem Pro Partner, we offer products with some of the best warranties and performance ratings in the business — and the skills to maintain them over time. We recommend having all HVAC equipment inspected yearly and replacing air filters every 30 to 90 days.
