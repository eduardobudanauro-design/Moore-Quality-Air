Water Heaters

Your water heater works hard every day. When it is time for a repair or a replacement, we are the team to call. We install and service traditional tank water heaters for homes across Albuquerque and the surrounding area.

Tankless Water Heaters

Ready to upgrade to an on-demand hot water system? Tankless water heaters are energy efficient, long-lasting and deliver hot water exactly when you need it. We install and service tankless units from leading brands.

Faucets and Faucet Repairs

A dripping faucet wastes water and drives up your bill. We replace and repair faucets in kitchens, bathrooms and utility rooms. Fast service, clean work and no mess left behind.

Toilets

We install and repair toilets for homeowners across our service area. Whether you are upgrading a bathroom or dealing with a running toilet that will not stop, we handle it.

Hose Bibs

Exterior hose bibs take a beating from New Mexico weather. We install and replace hose bibs to keep your outdoor water access working year round.

Garbage Disposals

We install and replace garbage disposals quickly and cleanly. If your current unit has stopped working or you are upgrading your kitchen, we have you covered.

A Note on What We Do Not Do

We focus on what we do best. Moore Quality Air handles fixture plumbing for residential homeowners. We do not snake drains or sewer lines, repair slab leaks, or service commercial overhead sprinkler systems. If that is what you need, we will gladly point you in the right direction.
